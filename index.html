Conway's Game

Conway's game of life is a set of rules for cellular automata. Cellular automata is a system in which there is a grid of cells that are either fillled or empty depending on the states of the surrounding cells.

Heat Visualzation

In our heat visualization demo, you can type a city or zip code, and the background will move along a range from orange to blue depending on the heat of that perticular city. Along with the color changing, there are chaotic balls bouncing around the screen, at a speed determined, again, by the tempurature.

Tree Generator

Double Helix is a program that models the way a spinning double helix looks. You can change the height, color, speed, and width of the double helix.

Double Helix

To start, a random tree is generated in a fractal. Every tree of level n, has a line and then two smaller trees of level n-1, offset by an angle. The random branches come from the fact that the length of the branches decreases by a random number. In addition, the angle that the two trees are offset by are random angles

Land Generator

Land is random generator that creates landscapes of different cargories. You can choose between Only Ocean, Ocean, Only Land, and Continent. They all vary by grass percentage, sand percentage, water percentage, and forest percentage. You can reset to get another randomly generated landscape.
